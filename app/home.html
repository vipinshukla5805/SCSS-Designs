Get Started

Real language, real context

WordBrewery uses only real, recent, and relevant sentences pulled from newspapers around the world. No more computer-generated or canned phrases; no more isolated words and definitions.

The shortest path to fluency

The most common 2500 words in any language make up 95% of daily vocabulary. WordBrewery shows you only sentences with high-frequency words so you get the maximum return on your investment of time.

Learning tailored to you

Each learner can choose to see only sentences at a particular skill level and for a particular topic. The WordBrewery algorithm tracks your progress and selects sentences just above your current skill level.

For accelerated, intermediate, and advanced learners

WordBrewery's challenging but approachable content is ideal for language learners who want to refresh and expand existing knowledge or graduate beyond beginners' apps and start reading real articles in their target language.

Learn in short bursts

People learn best in short bursts, and our brief lessons fit into your busy life. Each sentence we select is packed with useful words, and even reading a few of sentences each day will bring you measurably closer to real fluency.

Inexhaustible content

There are hundreds of thousands of sentences in our database, and we are constantly gathering new ones and testing them for usefulness. This means you will never run out of new challenges.
